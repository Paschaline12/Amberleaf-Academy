Our Facilities

Play Ground

Vast playground in the school:

The playground at Amberleaf Academy is a vast and vibrant space designed to promote physical activity, teamwork, and recreation. With the capacity to host up to 1,000 students at once, it is a hub of energy and activity throughout the school day. The sprawling grounds feature a variety of facilities, including football fields, basketball courts, a running track, and dedicated areas for other sports like volleyball and tennis. For younger students, there is a safe and engaging play area with swings, slides, and climbing structures. Shaded seating areas surround the playground, offering a place to rest and socialize. The well-maintained grass fields and modern sports equipment ensure students have access to high-quality facilities for games and physical education. The Amberleaf Academy playground is more than just a recreational space; it is a cornerstone of the school’s commitment to holistic development, encouraging fitness, teamwork, and a love for outdoor activities.

Dining area

Great with meals and modern facilities

The dining hall at Amberleaf Academy is a spacious and welcoming facility designed to accommodate all students comfortably during meal times. With its modern design and a seating capacity for hundreds of students, it serves as a vibrant hub where students gather to enjoy nutritious meals and foster friendships. The hall is equipped with long tables and comfortable seating to promote communal dining, encouraging interaction and camaraderie. The kitchen, located adjacent to the dining area, is staffed by skilled chefs who prepare a variety of healthy, balanced meals that cater to diverse dietary needs. Hygiene is a top priority, with strict cleanliness standards maintained at all times. Natural lighting, colorful decor, and efficient ventilation create a cheerful and relaxing environment for students. Beyond being a place to eat, the dining hall fosters a sense of community and well-being, contributing to Amberleaf Academy’s holistic approach to student development.

Computer lab

We take IT skills seriously for all

The Amberleaf Computer Lab stands as a state-of-the-art facility designed to enhance digital learning and innovation. With its capacity to host over 300 students at a time, it provides an unparalleled environment for collaborative and independent study. Each workstation is equipped with high-speed computers, ergonomic furniture, and reliable internet connectivity, ensuring students have access to cutting-edge technology for research, programming, and creative projects. The lab also boasts advanced audio-visual systems, making it an ideal space for workshops, online lectures, and group discussions. Its spacious layout ensures comfort and accessibility for all students, fostering an inclusive atmosphere. The Amberleaf Computer Lab is not just a place for technical education but a hub for fostering creativity, problem-solving, and critical thinking. With its innovative design and modern equipment, it empowers students to thrive in an increasingly digital world, preparing them for academic success and future technological advancements.

Transport Network and facilities

We pick and drop at any location as per need.

Amberleaf Academy boasts a robust transportation network, featuring a fleet of 50 well-maintained buses designed to ensure safe and efficient travel for students. These buses provide comprehensive coverage, reaching every residential area within the city, making the academy accessible to families from all neighborhoods. Each bus is equipped with comfortable seating, air conditioning, and GPS tracking systems, ensuring a pleasant and secure journey for students. The academy prioritizes student safety, with trained and experienced drivers operating the buses and staff members on board to supervise younger students. A strict schedule is maintained, allowing punctual pick-ups and drop-offs, while parents are kept informed through real-time updates on routes and timings. Amberleaf Academy’s transport network not only reduces commuting stress for families but also minimizes the environmental impact by promoting shared travel. This efficient system reflects the academy’s commitment to convenience, safety, and sustainability for its students and their families.

Classrooms

Modern classrooms with a great environment

The classroom at Amberleaf Academy exemplifies modern educational excellence, blending functionality with a warm, inviting design. Spacious and well-lit, the room features large windows that allow ample natural light, creating an airy and vibrant atmosphere conducive to learning. Sleek wooden desks and ergonomic chairs are neatly arranged, promoting both comfort and focus for students. At the front of the room, a state-of-the-art interactive smartboard is the centerpiece, flanked by whiteboards designed for collaborative activities and creative exploration. The minimalist decor is complemented by colorful storage cabinets and motivational posters, fostering a positive and inspiring environment. Potted plants add a touch of greenery, promoting a calming and refreshing ambiance. Attention to detail is evident in features such as a digital clock and an efficient air conditioning system, ensuring a comfortable experience year-round. This thoughtfully designed classroom reflects Amberleaf Academy’s commitment to providing students with a cutting-edge and nurturing educational environment.

Modern Library

Has more than 400 sitting spaces

The library at Amberleaf Academy is a modern haven for knowledge and creativity, as depicted in the image. Its spacious and well-organized layout features tall bookshelves filled with a diverse collection of books, catering to students of all interests and academic needs. Large windows allow an abundance of natural light to flood the room, creating a warm and welcoming environment ideal for studying and reading. The library offers various seating options, including study tables arranged in clusters for group discussions and individual desks for quiet concentration. A cozy reading corner with comfortable armchairs and a soft rug near the windows adds a touch of relaxation, encouraging students to unwind with a book. The decor is inspiring, with motivational quotes on the walls and potted plants that enhance the tranquil atmosphere. With its serene ambiance, functional design, and ample resources, the Amberleaf Academy library is a true cornerstone of academic growth and intellectual exploration.
